FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers – and indeed in the history of international football – was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31–0.

This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal haul set a new world record for an individual player.

And though the defeat earned American Samoa ignominy, its recovery is now the subject of a documentary, 'Next Goal Wins'.
